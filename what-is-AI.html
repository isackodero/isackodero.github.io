Now let's define these terms:

Artificial Narrow Intelligence (ANI)
also known as weak AI, involves applying AI to a specific task eg. Siri, face verification on iPhone, Tesla autopilot, Sofia, and Google Maps.
Artificial General Intelligence (AGI)
also known as Strong AI, involves a machine that possesses the ability to perform any intellectual task that a human can perform.
Artificial Super Intelligence
refers to a software-based system or an agent with intelligence that surpasses humans across every comprehensive range of categories and fields.
